About

Hi, My name is Tharindu Weeraratne.

I love studying how biological systems work and apply that knowledge to create solutions for people and environment. I perform research, analyze data, create models and insights to help develop novel solutions.

At WayBeyond, I use crop science, cutting-edge technology and data science to uncover relationships between crop genetics, their environment and management practices. I collaborate with leading agriculture universities and research institutions. I love helping growers with novel crop monitoring, prediction, and management tools to produce better crops while minimizing the impact on the environment.

At Fluence Bioengineering, I contributed to develop new horticulture lighting technologies by discovering impacts of light on crops. I was involved in developing high intensity LED top light solutions for greenhouses and indoor farms, high intensity LED lighting solutions to grow high value crops full cycle in indoor vertical farms and LED lighting solutions for high density cultivation of leafy greens and herbs.

My areas of specialieties include Plant Biology, Crop Science, Agronomy, Horticulture, Biotechnology, Plant Pathology, Photobiology, Postharvest Biology and Technology and Data Science

I love sports, travelling, gardening and art! 😍
